Frequently Asked Questions

As you adjust the compression level or resize dimensions, ImageForge Pro immediately processes a low-resolution preview to show you the expected results. The final processing when you click "Process Image" will use higher quality algorithms for the best results.

AVIF and WEBP are modern image formats that provide superior compression compared to JPEG and PNG. They can reduce file sizes by 30-50% while maintaining similar or better quality. WEBP has wider browser support, while AVIF offers even better compression.
